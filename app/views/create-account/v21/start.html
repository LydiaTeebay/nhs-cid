What is an NHS login?

NHS login is a new way to log in to health websites and apps. If you do not have an NHS login you will need to set one up, and enter some information to prove who you are. This is to keep your health information secure.

We may take 12 hours to check your information because lots of people are using the service.

When you have an NHS login, you can use it to access health websites and apps with the NHS login button.
