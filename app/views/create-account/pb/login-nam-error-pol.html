We cannot log you in

The registration details you used to prove who you are have changed.

This may be because:

  • you have changed your GP surgery
  • your GP surgery has changed GP systems
  • your GP surgery has merged with another or split

Speak to your GP to get updated registration details or use photo I.D. to prove who you are instead.

Other websites and apps may allow you to log in without new registration details. Some may allow limited access until your details have been updated.
