What does NHS login do?

NHS login makes it easier and quicker for you to securely access multiple digital health and care services with one username and password. This simple, one-time sign-up process means you do not have to prove who you are each time you access a service.

What services can I access with NHS login?

NHS app

You can book GP appointments, manage your prescriptions and access a range of other healthcare services.

eRedbook

Keep track of your child's health, see upcoming appointments and read interesting articles about their development.

Patient Access

You can connect to your GP, book pharmacy services and access medical content.

You can use your NHS login to access these services, and anywhere else you see 'NHS login'.
