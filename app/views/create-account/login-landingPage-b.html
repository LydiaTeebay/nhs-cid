What does NHS login do?

NHS login lets you access multiple digital health and social care services whenever you want, with a simple, one-time sign-up process. This means you don't have to prove who you are each time.

What can I use my NHS login for?

You can use your NHS login to access services such as The NHS app. Once you are set-up, you can:

  • manage your prescriptions
  • book GP appointments
  • recording your child's development

You can also use your NHS login with a number of services, such as eRedbook, Helm and Patient Access.

What are the benefits of using NHS login?

NHS login is secure and can be used repeatedly without you having to prove who you are each time. NHS login follows the UK government's data storage standards to keep your information secure.
