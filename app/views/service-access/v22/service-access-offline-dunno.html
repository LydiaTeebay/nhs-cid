You can ask your GP surgery if you are registered for their online services

People who are registered for online services can use their registration details to prove who they are.

If you are not registered

If you are not registered for online services, you can register by filling in a form that your GP surgery will give you.

Your surgery will give you some registration details. You need all 3 of the following details:

  • Linkage Key (could be called Passphrase)
  • O.D.S. Code (could be called Organisation Code or Practice I.D.)
  • Account I.D.

You can use these 3 registration details to prove who you are instead of sending a photo of your I.D.

If you are registered

If you are already registered, ask your GP surgery for the 3 registration details you need for their online services. Make sure you get all 3 details.

You can use these 3 details to prove who you are instead of sending a photo of your I.D.
