Go to your GP surgery to prove who you are

Follow these steps:

  1. Tell your GP surgery that you want to sign up for their online services.
  2. Your surgery will ask you to fill in a form.
  3. Your surgery will give you registration details. You will need:
    • Linkage Key (could be called a Passphrase)
    • O.D.S. Code (could be called an Organisation Code or Practice I.D.)
    • Account I.D.
  4. Use your NHS login to access ’{{ serviceName }}’ and use these 3 details from your GP surgery to prove who you are.
