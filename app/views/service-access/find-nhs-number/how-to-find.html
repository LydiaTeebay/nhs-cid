How to find your NHS number

For security reasons we must verify who you are before you can find your NHS number. We will do this by searching for your contact details in our database.

    Tell us your name, date of birth, gender and postcode. We can then find your NHS number in our patient database.

  2. Receive a code

  3. Make your choice

    When your code is verified, you will be able to manage your choice online.
