Register to use your GP surgery's online booking system

If you register for your GP surgery's online booking system, you can use the details you'll get to help us find your NHS record.

This also proves to us that your GP surgery has checked who you are.​

Follow these steps:

  1. Ask your GP surgery to register you for their online services - they will give you registration details.​
  2. Log in to the NHS App using your NHS login.
  3. Use the details your surgery gave you so we can connect you to your NHS record.
