Cannot connect to your GP surgery

Reference: CID7023

The website or app you are trying to visit needs to connect to your GP surgery and it has not been able to do so.

This may be because:

  • you already have an NHS login with a different email address
  • you have changed GP surgery
  • your GP surgery has changed systems
  • you no longer have permission to use your GP surgeryâ€™s online services

  1. What you can do

    If you already have an NHS login, you cannot create another one.

    You should go back and log in again using the email address you registered with.

    Go back and log in

    If you want to change the email address on your NHS login, you can do so in the NHS login settings.

    Or

    If this does not work, ask your GP surgery for your registration details on their GP online services. These are different to your username and password.

    You will need:

    • account ID
    • ODS code (also called organisation code or practice ID)
    • linkage key (also called passphrase)

    When you have these, log in again and enter your registration details.
