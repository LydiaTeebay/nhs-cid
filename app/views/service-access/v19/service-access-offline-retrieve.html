You can ask your GP surgery for your registration details so you can prove who you are

Follow these steps:

  1. Ask your GP surgery for your registration details so you can use their online services. The 3 details you need are:
    • Linkage Key (could be called Passphrase)
    • O.D.S. Code (could be called Organisation Code or Practice I.D.)
    • Account I.D.
  2. When you have all 3 details, use your NHS login to access {{ serviceName }}. You can use the 3 registration details to prove who you are instead of sending a photo of your I.D.
Or

You can send a photo of your I.D. to prove who you are.
