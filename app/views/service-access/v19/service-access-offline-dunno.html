You can ask your GP surgery if you are registered for their online services

People who are registered can use their online services registration details to prove who they are.

If you are not registered

If you are not registered for online services, you can register by filling in a form that your GP surgery will give you.

Your surgery will give you some registration details. You need all 3 of the following details:

  • Linkage Key (could be called Passphrase)
  • O.D.S. Code (could be called Organisation Code or Practice I.D.)
  • Account I.D.

If you are registered

If you are already registered, you can ask your surgery for your 3 online services registration details.
