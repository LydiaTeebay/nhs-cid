You can register for your GP surgery's online services so you can prove who you are

Follow these steps:

  1. Tell your GP surgery that you want to register for their online services. They will ask you to fill in a form.
  2. Your GP surgery will give you registration details. You need all 3 of the following details:
    • Linkage Key (could be called Passphrase)
    • O.D.S. Code (could be called Organisation Code or Practice I.D.)
    • Account I.D.

  3. When you have all 3 details, use your NHS login to access {{ serviceName }}. You can use the 3 registration details to prove who you are instead of sending a photo of your I.D.
    4. {% else %}
  4. When you have all 3 details, go to the website or app you were trying to access. Use your NHS login. Then use the 3 registration details to prove who you are instead of sending a photo of your I.D.
    5. {% endif %}
