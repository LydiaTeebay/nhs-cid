Get your ID checked at a Post Office

You'll need to take one of the following types of I.D. to a Post Office:​

  • passport​
  • driving licence​
  • national identity card ​

How it works:​

  1. Help us find your NHS record – we'll ask you for some information.​​
  2. We'll help you find a Post Office where you can take you ID.​
  3. At the Post Office, they will take a photo of your ID to send to us. They'll also check you look like the photo in your ID.​
