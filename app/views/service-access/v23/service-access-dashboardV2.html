    How to find your NHS number

    Your NHS Number is a 10 digit number, like 485 777 3456.

    You can find it on any document that the NHS has sent to you, such as:

    • prescriptions
    • test results
    • hospital referral letters
    • appointment letters
    • your NHS medical card
