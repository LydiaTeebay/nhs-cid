Step 1 of 3 How you can help us

We are asking members of the public to help us choose a name for a new NHS service. We will firstly ask you to watch a short video, which explains the service, and then ask you to answer a few questions. The whole process takes about 5-6 minutes.

About the service

The service will allow people to use websites and apps that contain personal health data. Because of security needs people will have to prove who they are, in order to connect to their health record. The video shows how people will be able to do this. This would only be done once, and then the login details (email + password) could be used to enter several health websites and apps that include personal data.
