Step 1 of 3 How you can help us

We’d like you to help us choose a name for a new NHS service.

You’ll need you watch a short video about the service, and then answer a few questions about what you’ve seen. It will take about 5 minutes.

About the new service

The service will allow people to use the same email address and password for many different personal health websites and apps.

So that we can keep their health information safe, people who want to do this will need to prove who they are.

The video will show you how this will work for the NHS App.

After a person has set up their email address and password, and they have proved who they are, they’ll be able to use their secure details for other health websites and apps
