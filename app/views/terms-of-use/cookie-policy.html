Cookie policy

We put small files called cookies onto your device (for example your phone).

We use:

  • cookies that we need to put on your device for our platform to work
  • analytic cookies to collect information about how you use our platform to help us make improvements to it

We ask you to accept our privacy notice when you set up your NHS login. By accepting our privacy notice, you agree to let us put both the cookies we need to set for our platform to work and analytic cookies on your device.

Cookies we need to set

The cookies we need to put on your device for our platform to work include:

Name Purpose Expires
id_token

This is set when you log in.

It lets us know that you have logged in and tells us who you are so we can keep your information secure.

 When you close your browser.
nhs_authorization_cookie

This is set when you use our platform for the first time.

It stores information passed to us by the health websites and apps you are using your NHS login with. We use it to make sure only they can see your information.

It will only be set if the health websites and apps are approved by us when you try to use them.

 Deleted when you get to the service you came from.

Analytic cookies

We use Adobe Analytics software to collect information about how you use our platform to help us make improvements to it.

Adobe Analytics does not collect or store your personal information (for example your name or address) so this information cannot be used to identify who you are.

We do not allow Adobe to use or share our analytics data.

Adobe Analytics will be used to set the following cookies:

Name Purpose Expires
s_cc This lets us know if your browser allows cookies. When you close your browser.
s_sq This lets us know what the previous link you clicked on was. When you close your browser.
s_vi This lets us know your unique visitor ID time/date stamp that tells us the time and date you first visit our platform. 2 years.
s_fid This lets us know your unique visitor ID time/date stamp. It is only used if the s_vi cookie is blocked by your browser. 2 years.
AMCV_###@AdobeOrg This lets us know your unique visitor ID when you use our platform. 2 years.

Changes to this cookie policy

Our terms of use may change. We will inform you on the platform if we make any changes to our privacy notice, cookie policy or terms and conditions.
